A great way for early stage companies to grow quickly is by doing partnership deals to bring in new expertise and man-power in order to expand core competencies and gain market traction. We provide help to startups looking for strategic growth. We help define the value proposition, target customers, pitch deck, and business model. We build and manage a robust partner network to make connections, build relationships, and drive the discussions forward for your budding venture. We negotiate, we close, and we help you achieve success.


We 'offload' the heavy lifting and help you move faster and more efficiently.


The type of partnerships we syndicate include:

  • Co-Marketing / PR deals to gain exposure
  • Distribution deals to get you in front of more potential users (including publisher, content syndication, and affiliate deals)
  • API deals to get in front of more potential users, or to bring additional functionality into your product
  • Platform deals to get application developers to develop for your platform
  • Content acquisition deals
